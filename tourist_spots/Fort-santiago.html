Fort santiago

The fort is notable for being a headquarters of the armies of several foreign powers in Philippine history, including the Spanish (1571 to 1898), the British (1762 through 1764 during the Seven Years War), the Americans (1898 to 1946), and the Japanese (1942 through 1945). it is now a popular Tourist spot as it highlights the spanish architechture that was introduced to the philippines at the time.
